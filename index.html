OUR VISION

USHBA is an independent electronic music label based in Georgia, inspired by the raw landscapes of the Caucasus mountains and the uncompromising presence of Mount Ushba.

Founded with a focus on depth and integrity, USHBA is dedicated to releasing timeless techno rooted in structure, repetition, and subtle evolution. The label draws inspiration from artists who approach sound with discipline and precision — where atmosphere, tension, and rhythm unfold gradually rather than seek immediate impact.

Each release is carefully curated and developed with intention. We value clarity in sound, patience in progression, and music that holds its strength over time. Our catalog reflects a commitment to quality over quantity and substance over trend.

USHBA exists to support artists who share this mindset — those who create with purpose and view techno as a form of expression that is immersive, focused, and enduring.

In The Memory of

Nick Phaliani▼

This label is dedicated to the memory of our good friend, Nick Phaliani, who tragically lost his life during an expedition on Mount Ushba.

Nick was a true Svan — a remarkable skier, mountaineer, and mountain guide whose strength was matched by his generosity. In Mestia, he was a role model to many, known for his hospitality and his constant readiness to help others.

He welcomed everyone — locals, visitors from across Georgia, and foreigners discovering Svaneti for the first time. Through his spirit and work, he helped share the beauty of Georgia’s culture and wild landscapes with the world.

This label honors his legacy.
